1. LDA_NMF_Topics.json - Topic model results of LDA and NMF. Coherence score and keywords of topics are included.
2. berttopic_results_only_aspect_sentences.csv - Topic model results of BERTopic. Generated by 3_TopicModel_BERTopic_only_aspect_based_sentences.ipynb
3. ca_restaurants_used_labels.csv - labels used for filter california restaurants data
4. deberta_aspect_score_sentiment_results.csv - result of pre-trained Deberta model. Including aspect polarity and confidence score. Generated by Pretrained_Model_Deberta.ipynb. 
5. random_2k.csv - randomly get 2,000 samples from california dataset. Used for Setfit model and pre-trained Seberata model.
